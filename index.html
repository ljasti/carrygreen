About CarryGREEN

CarryGREEN is a bio-plastics manufacturing and research company focused on creating innovative, biodegradable, and compostable products. Guided by the expertise and training provided by the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), we are committed to designing sustainable solutions that address the environmental challenges posed by single-use plastics.

Through continuous research and development, we aim to produce high-quality products that add value to the planet.

As part of our collaboration with DRDO, we leverage cutting-edge advancements in biodegradable materials to replace conventional plastic products. This partnership enables us to create eco-friendly alternatives that decompose naturally within months, ensuring minimal environmental impact.
