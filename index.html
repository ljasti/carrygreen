About CarryGREEN

CarryGREEN is dedicated to creating high-quality, eco-friendly reusable bags made from natural fibers like jute and cotton. Our mission is to replace single-use plastics with durable, stylish alternatives.

We are a bio-plastics manufacturing and research company focused on innovating, designing, and developing differentiated bio-plastic, biodegradable, and compostable products. Our team of scientists and multi-disciplinary experts work tirelessly on sustainable and environmentally friendly solutions. Our commitment to incessant Research & Development drives us to create high-quality products that add value to the planet.

We are proud to partner with India's premier research organization, DRDO (Defence Research and Development Organisation), under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India, to replace single-use plastic products with compostable and biodegradable alternatives.
