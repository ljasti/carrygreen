Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Amrot different from other health drinks?

Amrot is a premium superfood blend made with carefully selected multi-grains, millets, pulses, nuts, and seeds. Unlike other health drinks, we focus on providing complete nutrition with natural ingredients and no artificial additives.

How should I prepare Amrot?

Mix 2-3 tablespoons of Amrot powder with warm milk or water. You can add honey or jaggery for sweetness. It can also be used in smoothies, porridge, or as a baking ingredient.

Is Amrot suitable for all age groups?

Yes, Amrot is designed to fuel every age, every day. It's suitable for children above 2 years, adults, and elderly people. However, consult your healthcare provider if you have specific dietary restrictions.

What are the main health benefits of Amrot?

Amrot provides complete nutrition, boosts energy levels, supports digestive health, enhances immunity, and helps maintain overall wellness with its rich blend of proteins, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

How long does one pack of Amrot last?

Depending on usage, one pack typically lasts 15-30 days for regular consumption. We recommend 2-3 servings per day for optimal benefits.

Are there any side effects or precautions?

Amrot is made from natural ingredients and is generally safe for consumption. However, if you have specific allergies to nuts, seeds, or grains, please check the ingredient list carefully. Pregnant or nursing women, individuals with medical conditions, or those on medication should consult their healthcare provider before adding Amrot to their diet.

Does Amrot contain any artificial additives or preservatives?

No, Amrot is made with 100% natural ingredients. It contains no added oils, no maida (refined flour), no eggs, no refined sugar, and no artificial preservatives. We focus on providing pure nutrition from natural sources.

Can Amrot replace my regular milk?

Amrot is designed to be a nutritious alternative to milk, especially in situations where pure milk is difficult to obtain. It provides a complete nutritional profile with proteins, carbohydrates, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals from plant-based sources.

What flavors of Amrot are available?

Amrot comes in multiple delicious flavors including Classic, Chocolate, Guava, Coffee, Spicy, Lemon, Elachi (Cardamom), and Beetroot. Each flavor maintains the same nutritional benefits while offering variety to suit different taste preferences.
